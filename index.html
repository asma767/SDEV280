TerRon Open

PRO / AM B-TIER

Kent, Washington

Directed by Tyler Shrock

The TerRon Open is hosted yearly at Lake Fenwick Park in Kent, Washington. This park boasts disc golf baskets alongside lush trees and greenery. It currently hosts 7 divisions and has been hosted since 2020.
